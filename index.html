My MSU memories

2020 is my third year in Michigan State University, and I have a great time in American, although I didn't have my family and old friends who accompanied me. it's my first photo in MSU,and I still remembered my first time to visit Michigan State University, everything was so beautiful and fresh for me, on the other hand, I didn't get any comfortable feel at that time,although new enveriment was attracted to me a lot. In fact, my life was filled of loneliness until I met new friends. I believed a lot of freshmen also met similiar questions. For me,friends takes an important part in my MSU memories, they not only brought me warm, but also taught me a lot. They let me know which dining hall is best, and they also took me to handle the phone card when I was a greenhand in American life. During MSU time, I think that my best benefit isn't knowledge in my memories, the real treasure is that I becaome a maturer and know how to live a unfamiliar place.
